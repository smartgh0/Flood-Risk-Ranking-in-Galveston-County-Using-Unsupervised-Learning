Visualization

Mapping with Folium and Matplotlib:

Objective: To visualize the distribution of flood risks across Galveston County by plotting the clustered data on a geographical map.

  • Plotted boundary maps of Galveston County with overlaid scatter plots of zip codes.
  • Annotated zip codes to provide insights on risk levels; providing clear visual indicators of high-risk areas.
  • Integrated custom legends and color mappings to differentiate between the clusters.
