Introduction

The objective of this project is to rank flood risks across various zip codes in Galveston County, Texas, using unsupervised learning techniques. By analyzing and clustering different risk factors, we aim to provide insights into areas that are more vulnerable to flooding, which can be crucial for urban planning, emergency preparedness, and policy-making.








Source: firststreet.org



Common Flood Risk Management Strategies

90%  Texas natural disasters are floods, costing an average of $254 million a year.



How This Project Tackle Flood Risk!

The unsupervised learning project successfully identified and ranked flood risks across Galveston County by clustering zip codes with similar risk profiles. The use of t-SNE for dimensionality reduction and K-Means for clustering proved effective in categorizing areas based on their vulnerability to flooding. The visualizations created provided clear, actionable insights that can inform decision-making processes for urban planning, disaster management, and policy formulation.
